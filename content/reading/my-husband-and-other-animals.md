This is not my usual genre. I don’t even remember how this book reached my Kindle. I wouldn’t have bought it or picked it up based on my usual reading habits. But somehow, I ended up reading it, and I’m glad I did. Part memoir, part science, with great observation, a good sense of humor, and unexpected encounters with wildlife. It was an easy, surprising, and enjoyable read.
