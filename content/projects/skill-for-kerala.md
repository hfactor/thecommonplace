Kerala flooded badly in 2018 and then again in 2019. The question was whether a community of creative professionals could do something more useful than a donation. Skill for Kerala was a platform where designers, illustrators, and writers pledged hours of their work. Clients found them, paid directly to the Chief Minister's Distress Relief Fund, and sent the acknowledgement back. The whole thing was built as a proper system, not a one-time campaign.

168 people pledged 3,646 hours across design, writing, workshops, and other work.
