---
title: Customers reveal reality
date: 2026-06-16
tags: [product, feedback]
---

An idea is only as good as what the world actually needs. Assumption and gut feel are not how products work. You can be opinionated about direction, but feedback is real data and opinions are not.

[[Focus on outcome, not output]] keeps this honest, the customer doesn't care about what shipped, only about whether it changed anything for them. [[Audience and customers are different]]: who responds to your work is not always who pays for it.
