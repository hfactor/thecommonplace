---
title: Write notes for yourself by default
date: 2021-06-08
tags: [notes, writing]
---

When you are making notes, they are for you. Don't focus on adjectives or writing style. Focus on context: when you read this months later, it should be clear what you meant. Never play with one-word or one-phrase notes. They lose meaning without context.

[[Writing the way you think]] is the same idea from the other direction, follow the association before worrying about how it reads.
