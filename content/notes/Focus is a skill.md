Focus is not a talent. It's something you build. The system that works for someone else probably won't work for you. Build your own. Compare your own data. Improve based on what you observe.

[[Tracking changes behaviour]], you can't improve what you don't measure. [[Systems emerge through use]], not from copying someone else's setup.
