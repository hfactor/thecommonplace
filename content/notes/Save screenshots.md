---
title: Save screenshots
date: 2021-06-04
tags: [tools, documentation]
---

Don't rename them, don't annotate them in the moment, just store them in a Year/Month folder and keep going. The value of the archive shows up later, not at the moment of capture. The habit of saving matters more than the habit of organising. Collect, even if you don't dissect.

[[Draw over the screenshot]] is one use. [[Hype Doc]] is another, both depend on having captured the moment before it passed.
