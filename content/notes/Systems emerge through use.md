---
title: Systems emerge through use
date: 2026-06-16
tags: [systems, productivity]
---

No system works for everyone. Most need small tweaks based on your role, your problems, and how you work. Don't force a system. Validate it. Follow it only if it's valuable. Work with data, check how it actually performs for you.

[[Borrow ideas not workflows]], start with someone's principle, not their exact setup. [[Tracking changes behaviour]] is how you know if the system is actually working.
