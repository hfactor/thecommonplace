---
title: Note titles are APIs for thought
date: 2021-06-30
tags: [notes, writing]
---

A good title compresses an idea down to something you can use in argument without reopening the full note. A claim-based title functions as a handle that slots into another note's argument naturally. A note that holds too many ideas produces a title that's either too broad to be useful or too narrow to be honest about what's inside.

[[Non-linear note making]] depends on this, connections between notes only travel if the title carries the idea clearly enough to work in a new context.
