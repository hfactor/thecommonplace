---
title: Small projects survive
date: 2026-06-16
tags: [product, shipping]
---

Stop building one big tool that solves everything. Be opinionated, find what the actual problem is, and build for that. Small projects thrive over time. Bigger initial versions exhaust the people building them. A conviction-driven, opinionated product is easier to pivot and easier to fail early with, which is a good thing.

[[Software design should be opinionated]] is the principle that makes this work, clarity of purpose is what keeps the scope from expanding.
