---
title: Good meeting checklist
date: 2021-08-09
tags: [meetings, communication]
---

A meeting without an agenda is a conversation, and sometimes that's what you need. More often it's a way of using everyone's time without a clear purpose. The minimum for a useful meeting: a stated goal, the right people in the room, and a record of what was decided.

[[Meetings are not the place to surprise people]], the agenda is also how you give people time to prepare. [[Documenting meetings]] is what turns the record into something usable.
