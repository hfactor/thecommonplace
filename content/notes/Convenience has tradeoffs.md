---
title: Convenience has tradeoffs
date: 2026-06-16
tags: [tools, systems]
---

Convenience is a surface feature. The thing that makes something easy to use in one direction often makes it harder to use in another. A tool that removes friction from the common path usually adds it elsewhere, in portability, in control, in what you can do when the convenient path doesn't go where you need.

[[Files outlive software]] is one version of this tradeoff: the most convenient app is often the one that owns your data.
