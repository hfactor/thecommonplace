We assume things can be delivered faster than they can. Check how you measure and how much you actually deliver. My assumption is still wrong. I need to add a buffer. Data is how you know.

[[Slam it shut and move on]] is the counter: at some point, ship what you have and learn from it rather than refining toward a deadline that keeps moving.
