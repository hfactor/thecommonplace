---
title: Question fiction like non-fiction
date: 2026-06-16
tags: [reading, learning]
---

Fiction deserves the same questioning you give non-fiction. Ask what the author is arguing, what the characters reveal, what the world of the book assumes. These questions take you to new theories and rabbit holes. For me as a non-academic person, it's a good way to learn.

[[Look for rabbit holes]], fiction is often a faster entry point than a paper.
