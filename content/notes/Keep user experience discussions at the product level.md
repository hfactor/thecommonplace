---
title: Keep user experience discussions at the product level
date: 2021-06-16
tags: [design, product]
---

UX decisions made only at the interface level optimise for surface over system. The real choices happen upstream: what the user is trying to do, where the product meets their life, what friction they're running into. These can't be resolved by adjusting a button. Pulling the conversation down to the interface too early is how you end up refining the wrong thing.

[[Solve the problem not the feature]], the problem only becomes visible at the level above the interface.
