---
title: Better thinking over better note-taking
date: 2021-07-23
---

The goal of a thinking system is not to capture more. It is to think better. Notes are useful because writing forces clarity and the archive gives you material to return to, but the notes themselves are not the product. The thinking is. [[Evergreen notes should be atomic]] gives that thinking a workable shape because narrow notes can be revised, contradicted, and reused without dragging a whole topic behind them.

What is hard is returning to the notes and letting them change how you understand something. [[Non-linear note making]] matters only when the connection changes the thought, not when the link makes the archive look richer. A large, well-organised collection that never gets used is filing under better conditions. The test is whether the note helps you think again later.
