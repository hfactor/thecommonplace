---
title: Save screenshots
date: 2021-06-04
---

Don't rename them, don't annotate them in the moment, just store them in a Year/Month folder and keep going. The value of the archive shows up later, not at the moment of capture. The habit of saving matters more than the habit of organising: organisation can happen later, but the moment can't be recovered. [[Draw over the screenshot]] requires material to work from, and building the discipline of capturing before moving on is what keeps that material available.

[[Hype Doc]] is one downstream use of this archive: screenshots give visual evidence of what shipped and how it evolved, in a way written notes alone can't. [[Notes grow in value when written to evolve]] applies here too: the screenshot from six months ago is more useful than the one from today, because it shows where you were before you knew where you were going.
