---
title: Zettelkasten as a modular architecture
date: 2021-07-16
---

A Zettelkasten is not a filing system. It is a structure for building knowledge from small independent pieces. Each note is atomic in the way [[Evergreen notes should be atomic]] describes: one idea, stated clearly, connected to others by genuine relationship rather than category. The modularity is what makes it generative. You can combine notes in ways you did not anticipate when you wrote them.

The system produces insight rather than retrieval when the links are earned. [[Non-linear note making]] is the practice that feeds it, but the link is not the point. The point is the collision that changes the thought. A folder structure stores what you already know; a good Zettelkasten keeps making the note unstable in useful ways.
