---
title: Non-linear note making
date: 2021-06-03
---

Categories create the illusion of organisation. Ideas stored by subject are easy to retrieve, but retrieval is not the hard part. A note about process design and a note about human psychology might be related, but they will not meet just because both were filed correctly. The hard part is making connections between things that do not obviously belong together.

[[Zettelkasten as a modular architecture]] describes the structure that makes this possible at scale: each note linked by genuine relationship rather than administrative category. [[Writing the way you think]] is the practice that feeds it. The risk is mistaking links for thinking. A connection only matters when it changes what the note can do.
