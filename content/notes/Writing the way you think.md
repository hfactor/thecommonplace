---
title: Writing the way you think
date: 2021-07-23
tags: [writing, thinking]
---

Ideas don't arrive in outline form, they come associatively, in fragments, out of sequence. A writing practice that forces linear order at the start creates friction with how thinking actually works. Writing the way you think means following the association before imposing structure. The structure comes later, in editing.

[[Non-linear note making]] is the system-level version: associative connections beat hierarchical ones because they match how knowledge actually forms.
