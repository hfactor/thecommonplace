---
title: Focus on outcome, not output
date: 2021-08-13
tags: [product, work]
---

Output is easy to measure: tasks completed, features shipped, documents written. Measuring output rewards movement over progress. You can satisfy every item on a task list while having no real impact on the thing that matters. Outcome requires knowing whether the work changed anything, which is a harder question and one that often doesn't get asked until after the work is done and the result disappoints.

[[Solve the problem not the feature]] is the upstream version of this, if you start with the outcome you're trying to change, the feature becomes a means, not a goal.
