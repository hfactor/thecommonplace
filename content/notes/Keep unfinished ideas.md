---
title: Keep unfinished ideas
date: 2026-06-16
tags: [ideas, notes]
---

Archive ideas instead of deleting them. An idea that doesn't fit now might make sense later when the context changes. The cost of keeping is low. The cost of deleting is permanent.

If an idea comes back, evaluate it then. Not everything in the archive deserves to be used. [[Notes grow in value when written to evolve]], the same principle applies to raw ideas, not just finished notes.
