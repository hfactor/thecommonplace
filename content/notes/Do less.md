---
title: Do less
date: 2021-07-03
tags: [focus, productivity]
---

Doing less is not the same as doing nothing. It's prioritising ruthlessly enough that the most important work gets real attention. Every time you add something to the list, something else gets less. The more useful question isn't what should I add, but what can I stop.

[[Focus requires subtraction]], the same logic applied to scope and decisions, not just the task list.
