title: Notes grow in value when written to evolve
date: 2021-06-09

status: active

A note written to be finished is less useful than one written to be returned to. Treating a note as a record stops the work at capture, and the archive becomes static. The compounding happens in revision: when you come back six months later and the note surprises you, disagrees with you, or needs updating, that friction is the system working.

[[Keep unfinished ideas]] follows from this, if an idea isn't ready yet, archive it rather than forcing it to resolution.
